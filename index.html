About LuxetileLLC

Founded in 2002, LuxetileLLC began with a passion for authentic Mediterranean tile artistry. Over two decades, we have partnered with European masters to bring unparalleled ceramic, marble, and porcelain to designers across Wisconsin and beyond.

Our dedication to sustainable luxury earned us the EU Certificate 1st Place — a recognition of our innovation, durability, and timeless aesthetic. Every tile tells a story of precision, and our family-led team ensures every project gets white-glove service.
